Fried Rice

Description

A procedure on how to make chinese stir fried rice.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • Chopped carrots
  • Chopped onions
  • Peas
  • Minced garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Oyster sauce

Steps

  1. In a pan, put oil
  2. Add eggs, carrots, onions, peas, and garlic, and saut√© until soft and cooked
  3. Add rice, soy sauce, and oyster sauce into the pan, and stir with the eggs and vegetables
  4. remove pan from heat
