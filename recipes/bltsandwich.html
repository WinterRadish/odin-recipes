BLT sandwich

Description

A procedure on how to make the perfect BLT sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Bacon
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Mayonaise
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. take a bowl and mix the mayonaise and mustard to make the sandwich sauce
  2. Bake the bacon until it is crisp
  3. Toast the 2 slices of bread
  4. Cut a piece of tomato
  5. Spread 1 Tbsp of sauce over the slices of bread
  6. Place a piece of lettuce on a slice of bread
  7. Place the slice of tomato ontop of the piece of lettuce
  8. Place the pieces of bacon ontop of the piece of tomato
  9. Place the last piece of bread ontop of the piece of bacon
